# Project Stargate: Historical Context & Modern Investigation Parallels

## Executive Summary

Project Stargate (1970-1995) was a classified U.S. government program focused on remote viewing and psychic espionage research. This document contextualizes the current investigation within the historical framework of Stargate, identifying parallels, methodologies, and institutional patterns that may inform ongoing research into [[Asymptote Network LLC]], [[SalmonCloud Ltd]], and related entities.

## Historical Project Stargate

### Overview

**Program Name:** Project Stargate (also: Grill Flame, Center Lane, Sunstreak, Stargate)
**Duration:** 1970-1995 (declassified 1995)
**Organizations:** CIA, Defense Intelligence Agency (DIA), Army Intelligence
**Focus:** Remote Viewing (RV) for intelligence gathering and surveillance
**Budget:** Estimated $20+ million across 25 years
**Participants:** Remote viewers, scientists, military personnel, institutional affiliates

### Key Elements

**1. Remote Viewing Program**
- Parapsychological research into extrasensory perception
- Development of systematic RV protocols and methodologies
- Training programs for remote viewers
- Operational applications in intelligence gathering

**2. Institutional Networks**
- Military-intelligence complex coordination
- Academic partnerships (Stanford Research Institute, later SRI International)
- Government laboratory involvement
- Corporate and private sector contracts

**3. Research Objectives**
- Understanding consciousness and perception
- Developing surveillance capabilities
- Exploring brain function and enhancement
- Evaluating psychic abilities for military applications

**4. Declassification & Legacy**
- Officially concluded: 1995
- Declassified documents: Public records available
- Scientific debate: Effectiveness and validity disputed
- Ongoing interest: Remote viewing community continues research

## Historical Lessons

### From Stargate Declassification

**Lesson 1: Government Research Programs Can Operate Classified**
- 25-year classified program without public knowledge
- Specialized oversight without full Congressional knowledge
- Personnel and resources compartmentalized
- **Implication:** Current operations may have similar secrecy

**Lesson 2: Methodology Can Evolve Beyond Original Intent**
- Stargate began as defensive; became offensive
- Research evolved as understanding deepened
- Applications expanded beyond original scope
- **Implication:** Current operations may have evolved significantly

**Lesson 3: Institutional Continuation After Official Conclusion**
- Stargate officially ended 1995
- Remote viewing research continues in private sector
- Personnel continue with private organizations
- **Implication:** Programs may conclude officially while continuing operationally

**Lesson 4: Declassification Reveals Only Official Record**
- Declassified Stargate documents are sanitized
- Complete picture requires connecting scattered evidence
- Private sector operations remain hidden
- **Implication:** Full investigation scope may be broader than available documents
