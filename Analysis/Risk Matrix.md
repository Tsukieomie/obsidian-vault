# Risk Matrix

## Risk Assessment Framework

### Risk Calculation
**Risk = Likelihood × Impact**

### Likelihood Scale
- **VERY HIGH (5)**: >75% probability
- **HIGH (4)**: 50-75% probability
- **MEDIUM (3)**: 25-50% probability
- **LOW (2)**: 10-25% probability
- **VERY LOW (1)**: <10% probability

### Impact Scale
- **CATASTROPHIC (5)**: Life-threatening, severe permanent harm
- **CRITICAL (4)**: Significant harm, long-term effects
- **HIGH (3)**: Moderate harm, medium-term effects
- **MEDIUM (2)**: Minor harm, short-term effects
- **LOW (1)**: Negligible harm

### Risk Levels
- **25 (5×5)**: CATASTROPHIC RISK
- **16-20**: CRITICAL RISK
- **9-12**: HIGH RISK
- **4-6**: MEDIUM RISK
- **1-3**: LOW RISK
