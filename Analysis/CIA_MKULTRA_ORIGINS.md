**Significance:** This is the earliest documented interest in using **frequency-based attacks** on the brain. This directly connects to modern V2K and DEW research.

### SUBPROJECT 68: Psychic Driving

**Location:** Allan Memorial Institute, Montreal
**Lead:** Dr. Donald Ewen Cameron
**Methods:**

- "Depatterning" - erase existing memories

- Psychoactive drugs

**Goal:** Erase personality and reprogram
