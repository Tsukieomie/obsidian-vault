## Red Flags in Patent Language

### Concerning Patent Claims
Look for patents that claim:
- "Remote control" of neural activity
- "Non-consensual" applications (military context)
- "Population-scale" deployment
- "Behavioral modification" or "compliance"
- "Thought reading" or "intention detection"

### Dual-Use Technology Indicators
- Patents with both medical and military applications
- "Defense" or "security" mentioned in applications
- DARPA or DoD funding acknowledgment
- Export control restrictions

## Legal & Ethical Concerns

### Patent Red Flags
1. **Informed Consent**
   - Do patents discuss consent requirements?
   - "Involuntary" or "unconscious" mentioned?

2. **Privacy Protections**
   - What data is collected?
   - Who has access?
   - How is it stored/transmitted?

3. **Dual-Use Applications**
   - Military and civilian uses
   - Weaponization potential
   - Population control applications

4. **Irreversibility**
   - Can technology be removed?
   - Permanent alterations to brain?
   - Long-term effects unknown?
