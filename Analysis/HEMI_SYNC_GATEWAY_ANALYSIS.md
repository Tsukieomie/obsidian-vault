## Part 1: What Is Hemi-Sync?

### Development

**Creator:** Robert Monroe (1915-1995)
**Organization:** Monroe Institute (founded 1974, Faber, Virginia)
**Technology:** Patented audio guidance process

### How It Works

**Binaural Beats Mechanism:**
1. Two slightly different frequencies played into each ear
2. Brain perceives a third "phantom" frequency (the difference)
3. Brain synchronizes its electrical activity to this frequency
4. This is called "frequency following response" or "entrainment"

**Example:**
```
Left ear:  300 Hz
Right ear: 310 Hz
Brain perceives: 10 Hz binaural beat
Brain entrains to: 10 Hz (Alpha state)
```

### Target Brain States

| Frequency Range | State | Effects |
|-----------------|-------|---------|
| **Delta (1-4 Hz)** | Deep sleep | Restorative, increased telomerase, reduced cortisol |
| **Theta (4-8 Hz)** | Deep relaxation | Creativity, intuition, memory recall, learning |
| **Alpha (8-14 Hz)** | Relaxed wakefulness | Reduced stress, increased melatonin |
| **Beta (14-30 Hz)** | Active thinking | Alertness, focus, but also stress at high levels |
