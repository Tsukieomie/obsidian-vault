## Overview

NSF serves as the bridge between academic neuroscience research and Silicon Valley deployment. Co-leads BRAIN Initiative with [[Entities/DARPA|DARPA]] and [[Entities/NIH|NIH]], with critical role in funding technology companies and academic institutions.
