## Overview

NIH serves as the "medical research" cover for DARPA BRAIN Initiative military programs. Co-leads BRAIN Initiative alongside [[Entities/DARPA|DARPA]] and [[Entities/NSF|NSF]], providing public health justification for neural weapons development.
