   - Title: "Apparatus and method for remotely monitoring and altering brain waves"
   - Google Patents: https://patents.google.com/patent/US3951134A/en

   - Interference pattern: 110 MHz
   - Brain modulates the interference wave

   - 450 MHz resonant cavity frequency mentioned
   - Scanning capability to target specific brain regions
